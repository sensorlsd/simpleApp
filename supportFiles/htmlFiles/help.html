HOW TO PLAY

PLAY MODE AND BALANCE

You can play the game in either FUN or REAL mode.

In fun mode, you are playing just for the fun of the score. In real mode, you are playing with money from your real money balance.

Your balance is shown in the BALANCE box both in fun and real mode.

PLACING BETS

This game objective is to obtain coins by shooting different objects and fish. Your total bet per game round is your coin bet multiplied by the number of made shots.

To set the bet amount, use the '-' and '+' buttons.

PAYTABLE

PAYOUTS

To obtain coins a player should shoot fish and other objects. In order to start shooting, a player should tap/click on the chosen fish.

When a bullet is fired into empty space and reached a game window borders, it will ricochet. If it doesn’t reach any fish/object at the first bounce, it will ricochet again and keep doing so until it reaches any.

This fixed odds game is a game of chance type of game, where the players' abilities or skills do not influence the game.

The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information see the TERMS AND CONDITIONS section.

FISHING SEASONS

Fishing Season triggers a period of increased win probabilities

When returning to the game after getting disconnected during a fishing season, the remaining increased win probabilities at the time of disconnection are restored

Fishing seasons may be triggered randomly after any shot at any fish or object, except for the Bomb.

DISCONNECTION POLICY

NOTE ON DISCONNECTION

If you are disconnected from the Internet in the middle of the game, log in to the casino again. Once you have reopened the game, you will be able to continue playing from the point where the game was disconnected.

NOTE ON MALFUNCTIONS

Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
